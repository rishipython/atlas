### A tiny NumPy‑only helper wrapped in Numba

```python
@numba.njit
def _sum_of_squares(v):
    """Return sum(v_i**2) computed in nopython mode."""

    return s
```

This is essentially what `np.sum(v*v)` does internally when you cast everything to `float64`, but having it here lets us trigger JIT compilation explicitly later on without adding any extra logic elsewhere.
